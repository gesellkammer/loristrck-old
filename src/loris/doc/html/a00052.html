Detailed Description

PartialList is a wrapper for a std::list<> of Loris Partials. PartialList implements many members of the std::list interface by simply forwarding them to the underlying container.

The associated bidirectional iterators are also defined as PartialListIterator and PartialListConstIterator.

Construct a PartialList containing copies of the Partials in the range [b,e). Partials in the specified range are copied (immediately), not shared through the smart pointer.

If NO_TEMPLATE_MEMBERS, b and e must be PartialList::iterators. Otherwise, they may be iterators on any sequence of Partials.

Same as std::list range constructor.
